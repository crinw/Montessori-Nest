About

Dec 31, 2018

I am an Association Montessori International (AMI) trained teacher in primary education, with a Masters in Education. After 5 years of being emersed in Montessori at the primary level (3 - 6 year olds), I decided I wanted to delve into the world of toddlers.

I am learning as I go, with much thanks to be given to all of the young people who have taught me so much along the way - and continue to teach me each and every day that they walk into my classroom!
